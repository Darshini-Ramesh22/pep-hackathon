   - Primary KPIs aligned with objectives

5. IMPLEMENTATION CONSIDERATIONS
   Address practical execution factors:
   - Resource requirements
   - Timeline considerations
   - Quality control measures
   - Contingency planning
   - Scalability options

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Create a cohesive, integrated strategy
- Ensure all recommendations are budget-realistic
- Align strategy with market opportunities
- Include specific, measurable goals
- Consider practical implementation challenges
